About Me

I am currently a full-time Research Assistant (RA) at Dept. LSGI , Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) under the supervision of Dr. Chengxiang Zhuge. My research work focuses on the deployment of electric vehicle (EV) infrastructure, modelling of complex charging behavior of EV users, fuel options for electric mobility, spatial big data analysis, and large-scale micro-simulation, aiming to provide more insights into smart mobility and electrification of transportation. Before working as a RA, I received my master's degree in Geomatics from PolyU and bachelor's degree in Land Resource Management from Southwest University, respectively.
